Scene Summary

The vehicle was parked and unattended overnight when fire developed. On arrival the engine compartment and forward cabin showed the heaviest fire damage. Burn patterns radiate outward from the driver-side engine-bay wiring harness, identifying the area of origin at the electrical system rather than the cabin or fuel system.

Origin & Cause Findings

  • Area of origin: driver-side engine-bay wiring harness.
  • Cause: electrical fault (arcing) in the wiring harness — accidental.
  • Accelerant testing: canine screen and lab samples negative — no ignitable liquids.
  • Pour patterns / trailers: none observed. Burn pattern is consistent with an in-place electrical origin, not an applied accelerant.
  • Forced entry / tampering: none. Doors and ignition intact; no pry marks.

Classification

Based on origin, cause, and the absence of accelerants or tampering, this fire is classified as ACCIDENTAL. There is no physical evidence of an intentionally set or staged fire.
